Who I Am

Shakina Ray

Tan’si. I am Misty. My spirit name is Shakina Ray. I am First Nations — Nehiyaw and European, from Woodland Cree, Alberta, Canada.

I find fulfilment in empowering and inspiring people to fully actualize their power, voice, and inner healer. The work I do today comes from the deepest part of who I am — because the healer I am now is the one I needed growing up.

Registered Massage Therapist Neurofascial Specialist Emotion & Body Code Advanced PSYCH-K® Healing Guide Gridworker
I specialize in inner child healing, freeing the body of dis-ease and pain, and helping you remember you are the Creator of your reality.
